About Me

Who I am

My name is Eriyeza Buwembo and I am going into my third year of Computer science at the University of Toronto. I was born in Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada and am the youngest of 4 siblings. My parents were both born in Uganda and I visit there around every other year.

Interests

Basketball

I've played basketball for almost my entire life and was fortunate to be given the opportunity to play in University. Due to injuries, I decided not to continue playing competetively but I still love playing for fun with friends and in Intramurals. I also enjoy watching the NBA and my favourite player would have to be Kevin Durant.

Anime/Manga

I was first introduced to anime in grade 6 with Attack on Titan, but did not start watching actively until high school when my friends introduced me to One Piece. I got around 120 episodes in before my other friends started watching Naruto and I decided to watch with them. Naruto and Naruto Shippuden were the first anime that I completed. After completing them, I went back to One Piece and caught up to the latest episode at the time which was around 750. I was extremely eager to continue so I decided to start the manga where the anime left off and that was the beginning of my manga reading career. Since then I have watched a lot of different anime and started reading the manga of some anime that I really enjoyed. As of May 2020, my favourite anime of all time is Hunter x Hunter 2011.

Music

Being the youngest of 4 siblings, I grew up listening to a combination of the music that my older siblings listened to. My sister played the piano and I also played the violin and the viola until I started high school. These things largely shaped my music taste. My favourite genre to listen to is Hip Hop and R&B but I am open to listening to almost any music other than country music. I enjoy going to orchestras and the music score of a movie largely influences whether I'll like it or love it. My favourite Hip Hop Artists include Kanye West, Drake, Childish Gambino, Travis Scott, Frank Ocean, and many more. Notable film score composers I like are Hans Zimmer, Ludwig Goransson, and John Williams.
